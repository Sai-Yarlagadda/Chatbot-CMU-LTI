Q: What was Andrew Carnegie's pet?
A: Scottish terrier

Q: What is Carnegie Mellon University's official mascot?
A: Scotty

Q: Who co-chaired the Mascot Identity Task Force in November 2006?
A: The Task Force was co-chaired by Director of Athletics Susan Bassett and Dean of Student Affairs Jennifer Church.

Q: What percentage of students surveyed in February 2007 voted for the Scottish terrier?
A: 78 percent

Q: In the spring, who did the Task Force partner with to develop the graphics for the mascot?
A: SME Branding

Q: When did Carnegie Mellon's official mascot debut?
A: Nov. 10, 2007

Q: When was the official Scotty costume unveiled?
A: 2008 Spring Carnival
